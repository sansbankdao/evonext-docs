# Welcome To EvoNext

Discover **why EvoNext is transforming digital identity and transactions** in less than 5 minutes.

## Getting Started

Get started by **creating a new Dash Platform Identity** or try EvoNext immediately by signing in at **[EvoNext.app/connect](https://evonext.app/connect)**.

### Prerequisites

:::important Before you begin

- [Download DashPay](https://www.dash.org/download/) version 18.0 or above
  - **Important**: When installing DashPay, make sure to keep your seed phrase safe and secure
- Ensure you have sufficient Dash balance to cover identity registration fees

:::

## Creating Your Digital Identity

### Step 1: Generate a New Identity

Generate a new Dash Platform Identity using the **application onboarding process**. Your new Identity will be created immediately.

This process creates a unique identifier on the Dash blockchain that will serve as your digital identity for all EvoNext services.

:::tip Identity Registration

Your Identity will be automatically added to the EvoNext ecosystem after registration, enabling you to access all platform features.

:::

### Step 2: Register a Username

Register a unique username to make your identity easily recognizable across the EvoNext network.

```bash
# Example command (actual command will be available in the application)
evonext register-username "your-preferred-username"
```

:::note
Usernames are unique across the entire Dash Platform network. Think of your username as your digital fingerprint - choose wisely!
:::
