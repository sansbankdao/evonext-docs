### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

EvoNext is designed to prioritize user privacy while maintaining appropriate measures to prevent misuse for illicit activities. The platform itself does not directly handle traditional financial transactions but interacts with the Dash Network.

### Know Your Customer (KYC)

EvoNext prioritizes user privacy and does not require personal identification unless specifically required by law or when using certain integrated services that may have their own requirements.
